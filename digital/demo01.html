INSTALL ON MAC OS:
Version 1.0 (2020-01-17), 66 MB: PKG Installer, MacOS/OSX (Intel, Version 10.9+)
1) Double click the DoomHavenDemo intaller.
2) If you get a warning message from your OS telling you this application did not come from a certified Apple developer:
• 2a) In the Finder on your Mac, locate the DoomHavenDemo installer.
• 2b) Don’t use Launchpad to do this. Launchpad doesn’t allow you to access the shortcut menu.
• 2c) Control-click the app icon, then choose Open, from the shortcut menu. Click Open.
Note: The app will now be saved as an exception to your security settings,
you can open it in the future by double-clicking it, just as you can any Apple registered app.
3) The installer will guide you and install the game. You will need your admin password to install.
4) A shortcut called "DoomHaven" for the game will be on your desktop, double click it to play the game!

INSTALL ON WINDOWS:
Version 1.0 (2020-01-17), 58 MB, EXE Installer, Microsoft Windows (x64)
1) Use the Installer to install the program to the C Drive
2) Double click on the DoomHaven_Demo shortcut on your Desktop to play the game,
or click on DOOMHAVEN_DEMO01.wad once set up.

FEED is a collective project and curatorial platform.
